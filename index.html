About

Zhaolin Wang is a PhD candidate at the School of EECS, Queen Mary University of London under the supervision of Dr. Yuanwei Liu. His current research interests include next generation multiple access, integrated sensing and communication, and optimization theory.

He received the BS degree with First Class Honors from the joint programm of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) and Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) in 2020 and received the MS degree with Distinction from Imperial College London in 2021.
